#SMOG: Serving the Mysteries of GOD:

Christianity is not a Journey to Victory, but Life after Victory! At the outset, being the Light of the World, it is our utmost responsibility to unfold the Mysteries from the Word of God for the salvation of the World. Mysteries like We Carry God, We are an Epistle of GOD written by the Holy Spirit make us realize that we are the most precious in all of His creation and His very habitation redeemed by the sacrifice of Christ Jesus on the Cross. Our passion and burden are unraveling these mysteries to the Mankind, enabling Every Believer to get closer to God and live with the true potential, knowing the very intent and heart of God.
