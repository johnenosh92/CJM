ABOUT US

Our Mission is to be Ambassadors of Christ (2 Corinthians 5:20) and Reconcile Man with God through the Word of God. By making them realize that they are New Creature in Christ and the Old has passed away and all things have become new in Christ Jesus. To show how and why God created Man and what God intended Man to Live like. We believe that, as He is so are we in this World!

Herein is our love made perfect, that we may have boldness in the Day of Judgment: because as he is, so are we in this world

1 John 4:17

“For this is the covenant that I will make with the house of Israel after those days, saith the Lord; I will put my laws into their mind, and write them in their hearts: and I will be to them a God, and they shall be to me a people: And they shall not teach every man his neighbour, and every man his brother, saying, Know the Lord: for all shall know me, from the least to the greatest. For I will be merciful to their unrighteousness, and their sins and their iniquities will I remember no more.”

Hebrews 8:10, 11, 12

What We Believe.

We believe in one God who manifests as Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

We believe in exercising the Power of the Kingdom of God through divine healing, Miracles, signs and wonders through faith in the Name of Jesus Christ.

We Believe, that We Carry GOD as we are the Living Temple of God!! He dwells in Us walks and talks through us. We are His People, and He is Our God.

“for ye are the temple of the living God; as God hath said, I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they shall be my people.”

2 Corinthians 6:16

We practice the will of God that none shall perish:

"The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is longsuffering to us-ward, not willing that any should perish, but that all should come to repentance." ... He does not want anyone to be destroyed, but wants everyone to repent."

2 Peter 3:9

preaching the Gospel, Exact Word of God as is and as it is in the Bible.

We believe that Salvation is a Feast, we are all meant to Celebrate Life in Fullness as Kings and Priests which Father, Son and the Holy Spirit has lavished wholeheartedly, intentionally and perpetually for those who believe on Christ Jesus, the Heritage of every Christian.

We believe Christ Jesus Eternal Ecstatic Love, Grace and Goodness are Impeccable, Incredible and are so Indescribable yet so REAL and this is the Gospel of Goodnews!

We believe that Bible is the True Expression of God’s Love for the entire mankind, and what Christ Jesus has done on the Cross is the Ultimate Sacrifice for the redemption of mankind throughout ages.

But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us.

Romans 5:8

We execute as Stewards of the Mysteries of GOD expounding the Truth from the Word of God for the church, to understand the Heart and the very mind of Christ Jesus.

Let a man so account of us, as of the ministers of Christ, and stewards of the mysteries of God.

1 Corinthians 4:1

We strongly believe that New Covenant (Hebrews 8: 10, 11) enable every believer to know and understand Christ Jesus from the Word of God with the help Holy Spirit. (1 John 2:27 KJV)

We believe in water baptism and the baptism of the Holy Spirit. Also, in the gifts of the Spirit, and fruit of the Spirit which is Love. These are the common rights of every believer who are born above.

We believe Christ Jesus has come in Flesh as a seed of the Holy Spirit conceived by a Virgin, His Death on the Cross and Resurrection on the Third day and His Promise of coming again.

We believe, we are the living epistle of God written by the Holy Spirit. We practice Purity Holiness and Righteousness, as the world reads us and gets to know Our Heavenly Father.



Our Core Values .

Excellence

We strive to deliver excellence in all that we execute, preach, serve and live by the code of Excellence.

Honour

- This is our Core value we abide by our lives, we Honour God in everything not just with Our Tithes, Offerings and giving but, living a Life before God, with all of our heart, soul and body.

Saving the Lost

The will of God is that none should perish, and we live by this passion to let all the world know, experience and live with Him all eternity: "The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is longsuffering to us-ward, not willing that any should perish, but that all should come to repentance." ... He does not want anyone to be destroyed, but wants everyone to repent." (2 Peter 3:9). We strive to reach the unreached by publishing the goodness of God that leads to repentance.

Helping the Needy

Love thy Neighbour as thyself by which we fulfil all the Law! We love and live helping the needy as it is a great privilege for anyone to lend the Creator by Clothing the Naked, Feeding the Hungry, Sheltering the Destitute. We deliberately desire to serve the needy as how we would be doing it to Christ Jesus Himself.
